Student Advocacy

At Jefferson, students have rights and responsibilities that they are guaranteed as a part of their educational experience. The Student Advocacy Guidlines were created to help students be aware of how they can proactively manage their learning, self-advocate, and succeed in school.
Use this form to report divergences from the Student Advocacy Guidelines. Submissions will be kept strictly confidential, but name & grade are required for follow-up of the situation.
